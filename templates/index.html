CrowdSec

A modern Fail2ban using crowd sourced detection.

What we like: Follows local log files to build a profile of normal activity. Can take configurable actions e.g. ban IP, on detecting unusual behaviour. Crowdsources definitions of "unusual" without sending any log data to the cloud.

What we don't like: Early release. Open source but built by a startup that is on a route to funding next year.
